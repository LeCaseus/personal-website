I'm a Biomedical Engineering grad from Zamboanga City, now based in Wellington, New Zealand. During my internship at Zamboanga City Medical Center I got to do the real thing, keeping clinical equipment running, calibrating machines that people's lives depend on, and even designing 3D printed parts for the department.
