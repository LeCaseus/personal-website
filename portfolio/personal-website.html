why from scratch?

The original version of this site was built by pure vibe coding — a backend with Node.js, Express, a Postgres database, and Cloudinary for images. It worked, but I had no idea how. That felt wrong for something that's supposed to represent me.

So I stripped it all back to what I actually understand: HTML, CSS, and a small amount of JavaScript. From here, I'll add complexity back one piece at a time — only when I've learned what that piece does and why it's needed. The site will keep changing as I learn, and that's kind of the point.
