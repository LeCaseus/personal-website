To all Biomedical Engineering students

If I could go back and tell myself one thing, it's to learn AI development. Healthcare is one of the hottest spaces for it right now. Wearable remote monitoring, medical imaging — these areas are exploding and they need engineers who get both the biology and the tech.

You're already studying the right thing. Just add AI to the mix and you'll be ahead of the curve.
